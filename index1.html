I'm really planning to join a club soon or later so I can enhance my skills and perhaps join tournaments and win games! Table tennis is a special sport because it's like tennis but in a table. You don't have to run a lot or atleast as much as playing normal tennis, another thing is you have to learn something called spin. Spinning the ball in table tennis is super important because it gets your playing to the next level, and in order to have good spin you need two things. The first one is practicising and the second one is having good tools. If you don't have good tools then you can't make spin at all or just make a little that it's even considered as having zero spin! The posture is very important thing in order to enhance your skills mroe and get better. Without it you won't get effective shots and you will end up losing. The best place to improve everything and lots mroe are clubs. Clubs are very good way to improve your skills with speed and get into tournaments in no time.
